# IPL Analytics: Exhaustive Use Cases

A comprehensive catalog of questions, insights, and features a cricket fan might want from an AI-augmented IPL analytics site. Organized by user journey phase.

---

## Scenario 1: Pre-Match (Match is today, hasn't started yet)

### 1.1 Team Form & Momentum
1. What is each team's recent form? (Last 5 match results, W/L streak)
2. How has each team performed in this season so far? (Wins, losses, NRR, position)
3. Which team has momentum — who won more recently and more convincingly?
4. How do these teams perform after a loss vs. after a win? (Bounce-back tendency)
5. Is either team on a historic winning or losing streak?

### 1.2 Head-to-Head Record
6. All-time head-to-head record between these two teams
7. Head-to-head record in this specific season
8. Head-to-head record at this specific venue
9. Head-to-head record in recent years (last 3 seasons) — more relevant than all-time
10. Biggest wins by either side against the other
11. Last 5 meetings — results, scores, and standout performances

### 1.3 Venue Analysis
12. Average first-innings score at this venue (overall and this season)
13. Win percentage batting first vs. chasing at this venue
14. How does this venue behave across phases? (Powerplay-friendly? Death-overs graveyard?)
15. Highest and lowest totals at this venue
16. Does the pitch slow down in the second innings? (Average run rate by innings)
17. Spin vs. pace performance at this venue (economy, wickets, average)
18. Dew factor — does chasing become significantly easier at night at this ground?
19. Boundary count trends at this venue (is it a big-hitting ground or a placement ground?)
20. How does this venue compare to league average on key metrics?

### 1.4 Toss Analysis
21. Toss impact at this specific venue — does winning the toss matter here more than average?
22. What do captains usually choose at this venue? (Bat first vs. field first split)
23. Win percentage when choosing to bat vs. field at this venue
24. Has the toss preference at this venue changed over the years?

### 1.5 Player Matchups & Key Battles
25. Top batter vs. top bowler matchup history (e.g., Kohli vs. Bumrah — balls faced, runs scored, dismissals)
26. Which bowlers from Team A have the best record against Team B's top order?
27. Which batters from Team B have historically dominated Team A's bowling?
28. Left-arm spinner vs. right-handed batter matchup data for specific players
29. Powerplay specialists — who are the most dangerous openers in today's matchup?
30. Death overs matchup — whose death bowlers are better vs. the other team's finishers?

### 1.6 Player Form & Fitness
31. Key players' recent form (last 5 innings — runs, strike rate, balls faced)
32. Key bowlers' recent form (last 5 spells — overs, economy, wickets)
33. Are any key players returning from injury? What was their pre-injury form?
34. Player performance at this specific venue (career stats broken down by ground)
35. Which player has the best record in this fixture historically?
36. Players on milestones watch (approaching 5000 runs, 200 wickets, etc.)

### 1.7 Team Composition & Strategy
37. Likely playing XI for each team (based on last match + known changes)
38. Impact of any squad changes — what does dropping/adding player X mean for team balance?
39. Batting depth comparison — which team's lower order is more reliable?
40. Bowling variety comparison — pace/spin mix, left/right arm options
41. Overseas slot allocation — are both teams optimizing their 4 overseas picks?
42. Impact player usage patterns — how has each team used the impact player sub?

### 1.8 Predictions & Betting Context
43. Win probability based on historical data (form, venue, H2H, toss tendencies)
44. "If Team A wins the toss and bats, their win probability is X% based on venue history"
45. Key factors that could swing the match — what are the 3 things to watch?
46. "Upset alert" — is the underdog in a better position than the odds suggest?
47. Fantasy cricket picks — who are the differential/value picks for this match?

### 1.9 Context & Stakes
48. What does this match mean for playoff qualification? (Points table implications)
49. If Team A loses, are they eliminated? What NRR scenarios exist?
50. Is this a "dead rubber" for either team?
51. Historical performance under pressure — how do these teams play in must-win games?

## Scenario 2: During the Match (Live)

### 2.1 Run Rate & Scoring Context
52. Current run rate vs. par score at this venue at this stage of the innings
53. Projected total based on current run rate and venue history
54. "Teams scoring X/Y at Z overs at this venue have gone on to score [range] on average"
55. Phase-by-phase comparison: how does this powerplay score compare to venue/season average?
56. Required run rate for the chasing team (if second innings)
57. "The last time a team was at this score at this stage here, they scored [total] and [won/lost]"

### 2.2 Win Probability
58. Live win probability based on current match state (score, wickets, overs, venue)
59. How much did the last over/wicket shift the win probability? (Momentum meter)
60. "Team A's win probability has dropped from 65% to 42% in the last 3 overs" — momentum shift alerts
61. Win probability comparison: "At this stage, chasing teams at this venue have won X% of the time"

### 2.3 Player Performance in Context
62. "Kohli averages 52 at this ground — he's currently on 35(24), tracking above his venue average"
63. Current batter's record in this phase (middle overs, death overs) at this venue
64. Current bowler's economy in this phase vs. their season average
65. "This bowler has never been hit for a six in the powerplay this season" — streaks and records
66. Batter vs. current bowler head-to-head stats (every ball they've faced each other)
67. "Player X has scored 80% of his runs on the off side today — is the field set right?"

### 2.4 Bowling Analysis
68. Which bowler should bowl next? (Based on matchup data against current batters)
69. Current bowling spell analysis — line and length patterns, dot ball percentage
70. "This bowler's economy in overs 16-20 is 11.2 — captain should think twice about using them at death"
71. Remaining overs allocation — optimal way to distribute remaining overs among bowlers
72. Part-timer opportunity: "The current batter struggles against off-spin — why not give X an over?"

### 2.5 Historical Comparisons
73. "The last time Team A was 85/4 at 12 overs at Chinnaswamy, they scored 156 and lost by 22 runs"
74. "Only 3 times in IPL history has a team chased down 200+ at this venue"
75. "This is the highest powerplay score at this venue this season"
76. "No team has successfully defended a total under 160 at this ground in the last 2 years"

### 2.6 Momentum & Turning Points (Real-Time)
77. Dot ball pressure index — consecutive dots creating a building pressure situation
78. Boundary drought alerts — "Team A hasn't hit a boundary in 18 balls"
79. Wicket cluster detection — "3 wickets in 2 overs — this is a collapse pattern"
80. Partnership tracker — "This 67-run partnership is the highest 4th-wicket stand at this venue this season"
81. Over-by-over scoring comparison with first innings (if second innings)

### 2.7 Tactical Suggestions
82. "Based on the current required rate of 10.5, the batting team needs to target at least 2 boundaries per over"
83. "Historically, teams in this position accelerate after the 15th over — expect a gear shift"
84. "The chasing team should target bowler X who has an economy of 9.8 in death overs"
85. Field placement suggestions based on batter's wagon wheel data

### 2.8 Second Innings Specific
86. Chase tracker — are they ahead or behind the par chase curve?
87. "Teams chasing this exact target at this venue have won X out of Y times"
88. Required run rate by phase — what needs to happen in each remaining phase?
89. "The asking rate was 8.5 at the halfway mark in successful chases here — they're at 9.2"
90. Dew impact — "Second innings economy rates are typically 0.8 runs/over worse at this venue"

95. Was the toss decisive? What would have happened if the other team won it? (Counterfactual)

### 3.2 Player Ratings & Awards

### 3.3 Statistical Highlights

103. Milestone achievements (player's 50th IPL wicket, 3000th run, etc.)

### 3.4 Standings & Playoff Implications
105. Updated points table after this result
106. How did this result change each team's playoff probability?
107. NRR impact — how much did this margin of victory/defeat move the NRR needle?
108. "Team A now needs to win X of their remaining Y games to qualify" — scenarios
109. Which upcoming fixtures become "effective knockouts" after this result?
110. Elimination watch — is any team mathematically eliminated after this result?

### 3.5 Comparative Analysis
111. How does this match compare to the reverse fixture earlier this season?
112. "This is the 5th consecutive win for Team A at this venue — they own this ground"
113. Comparison with similar historical matches (same teams, same venue, similar context)
114. Season progression — is this team getting stronger or weaker as the season goes on?

### 3.6 Tactical Debrief
115. What went wrong for the losing team? Data-backed tactical analysis
116. Did the losing captain make any questionable bowling changes? (Based on matchup data)
117. Was the batting order optimal? "Player X batted at #5 but his strike rate is 150 at #3"

## What Makes This Different from Cricbuzz/ESPNcricinfo

| Traditional Stats Site | This AI-Augmented Site |
|---|---|
| Shows you the data | Tells you what the data *means* |
| Requires you to know what to look for | Surfaces insights you didn't know to ask about |
| Static tables and charts | Conversational — ask follow-up questions |
| Same view for everyone | Context-aware (knows the match, venue, stakes) |
| Backward-looking (what happened) | Forward-looking (what's likely to happen, and why) |
| Isolated stats | Connected narratives (links form, venue, matchup, stakes into a coherent story) |
| You need cricket knowledge to interpret | AI explains significance for any knowledge level |
| Generic analysis | Franchise-level strategic framing ("if you were the coach, here's what you'd want to know") |
